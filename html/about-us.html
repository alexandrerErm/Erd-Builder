About us

This website was developed as part of a thesis project, undertaken by UoM students Ermidis Alexandros and Antoniadis Leivadas Dionysios. It satisfies the need for a free - opensource fully functional diagramming tool for Entity Relationship diagrams.
The project was carried out during 2020/2021 academic year and was supervised by UoM proffesor: Evangelidis Georgios.
